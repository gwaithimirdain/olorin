Olorin Grades Download

Enter your course ID and password to download all grades in CSV format, which can be imported into a spreadsheet program like Excel or LibreOffice. The columns will be the level names in "world-stage-level" format, the rows will be labeled by student email addresses, and each entry will be 0 if the student has not completed that level, and otherwise 1, 2, or 3 according to whether they have completed it on novice, adept, or master difficulty setting.
